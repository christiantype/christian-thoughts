Christian Solorzano is a Graphic Designer from Chicago.

His work employs strategic solutions centered around collaboration and a deep understanding of client needs.

“I’m devoted to honest work that tackles important issues. Through collaboration and communication, I translate ideas and concepts into realistic solutions. My work focuses on design as a process that questions and pushes boundaries. Design as a vocation enables me to better understand the world and allows me to create honest work. I'm a believer that design can significantly improve our world and I aim to create work that makes a true impact.“

“My work is influenced by design history, advancements in technology and media, psychology, and kindness.”
