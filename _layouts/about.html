Introduction

I‘m Christian, a designer based out of Chicago.

Currently, I am a product designer at PwC, where I design product features and enhancements that improve the employee experience for thousands of PwC's employees. I co-design with stakeholders, strategists, and technologists, to ensure that our solutions are met with excitement, utility, and beauty.

In 2020, I founded the Chicago Graphic Design Club as a way to build community in Chicago centered around the theory, craft, and practice of graphic design. Since 2020, we‘ve hosted numerous workshops, tours, lectures, and in 2022, I launched our podcast, Underscore, where I speak with Chicago’s creative leaders on a range of topics.

As of 2022, I teach at DePaul University’s College of Computing & Digital Media. My teaching philosophy is to make the classroom a space where students are free to make mistakes, experiment, and develop critical thinking. My goal is to help students develop their unique perspectives and rationale behind the decisions that drive their work.

In addition, mindfulness and writing play a large role in my approach and relationship with design so on occasion I teach mindfulness at the Zen Life & Meditation Center of Chicago and I also write a biweekly newsletter titled Beginner's Mind, which explores and examines the margins of culture and creativity through a contemplative lens.
