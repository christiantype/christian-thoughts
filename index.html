Christian Solorzano is a Designer.

I'm devoted to crafting human-centered experiences with an emphasis on interaction-design and the user experience.

I currently live in Chicago, where I craft digital experiences at PwC's Experience Center. I've worked for non-profits, educational instutions, and advertising agencies.

I spend my free time reading and writing, managing my design publication, Faculty, making art, and taking photographs.

My work is influenced by design history, advancements in technology and media, psychology, and kindness.
