## Our first event
In 2020 we hosted our very first book discussion plus our first online lecture. This was the catalyst that encouraged us to continue offering accessible program to our community.
<img src="/assets/images/cgdc/cgdc-1.jpg">
<hr>
## Research
A Google Form was communicated via social media channels asking for input on the programming the community wishes to see more of. The form is always accessible on our website and our team uses it to validate and plan events and content.
<img src="/assets/images/cgdc/cgdc-8.jpg">
<hr>
## Branding
The Chicago Graphic Design Club's image is designed to have an attitude of playfulness and experimentation. Because we are focused on graphic design, we regularly refresh our typography and color but mantain a consistent framework around the updates we made.
<img src="/assets/images/cgdc/cgdc-9.jpg">
<figcaption>Examples of social media posts.</figcaption>
<img src="/assets/images/cgdc/cgdc-10.jpg">
<figcaption>We have accent and base fonts which are expressed throughout our communication. Often, we see a new typeface that we like, and we might swap out the accent. Same with the color — we have three primary colors that we use — accent, dark, and light — which we use to create a variety of gradients. Because we are an independent organization, we don't want to create constraints in how we express ourselves.</figcaption>

### A website that accommodates
Our website was designed to scale and accomodate future content. The majority of our content consists of programming but as of 2022, we are beginning to incorporate an editorial branch of the website. The website has been a great playground to explore and push our brand.

## A thriving and growing online community
A core pillar of ours is to provide on-going support and a sense of belonging amongst our community. Our Slack group is a space that is open to the public where design students, enthusiasts, professionals, and educators, can engage in conversation. We offer a range of groups that include career development, events, networking, and more.
