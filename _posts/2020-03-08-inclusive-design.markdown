## Why this is important to me
As designers of products — I believe it's our duty to acknowledge the distinct nature of people's experiences. Although this project isn't *visual design*, it granted me the opportunity as a designer, to step back, look at the bigger picture, and go beyond my role.

Throughout this process, I had the opportunity to speak with various discipline leads to understand the question:

*How can we hold ourselves accountable and increase our impact by taking into account real people with real problems.*
<hr>

#### Impact of this work
Since the release of this playbook, a professional development program has been initiated to upskill staff on inclusive design, where I've been serving as the SME. Inclusive design has now become a firmwide initiative.

## Thoughts
This has been by far my favorite project to work on. It highlighted the value of research, empathy, and caring for the people that engage with our products and services. Since then, inclusion has been something that I strongly advocate for on engagements and I've become an SME on the subject.
